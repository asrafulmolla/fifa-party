What is FIFA Party BD?

FIFA Party Bangladesh is an interactive community map platform designed for the FIFA World Cup 2026. It enables fans across Bangladesh to discover local watch parties, cafe projection spots, and rooftop screenings, browse real-time BST matches, check-in to locations, and share screening vibes.

How to Add a Venue?

Sharing a watch party spot with your fellow fans is easy:

  • Log in or register your profile.
  • Click **"Add Venue"** on the filter bar or navbar.
  • Fill in details (name, capacity, entry fee), pick the exact coordinates on our interactive map picker, select the matches being screened, and submit!

Verification & Points

Earn **Fan Points** and climb the leaderboard rankings:

  • **+10 pts** for submitting a new watch spot.
  • **+1 pt** for checking in ("I'm Going") to a match screening.
  • **+2 pts** when someone else checks in to your spot.
  • **+25 pts** + **Verified check badge** once your venue reaches community check-in thresholds!
